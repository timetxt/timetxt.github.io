极简字体排版 Minimal Typography

字体排版是安排字体的艺术和技术，使书面语言在显示时既易读、易懂，又具有吸引力。良好的排版建立了强大的视觉层次结构，提供了视觉平衡，增强了可读性。 Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. Good typography establishes a strong visual hierarchy and provides a graphic balance to enhance readability.

在极简设计中，字体排版成为焦点，通过精心关注字体大小、间距和微妙阴影来创造深度，而不会使内容过于复杂。 In minimal design, typography takes center stage with careful attention to font size, spacing, and subtle shadows that create depth without overwhelming the content.

大字体与精心的间距结合创造视觉冲击力，同时保持优雅和可读性。 Large type sizes combined with careful spacing create visual impact while maintaining elegance and readability.

空间的力量 The Power of Space

留白（负空间）与文本本身同等重要。宽裕的边距和行间距让内容呼吸，提高理解度。正确的平衡创造节奏，自然引导读者的视线穿过内容。 White space (negative space) is as important as the text itself. Generous margins and line spacing allow content to breathe and improve comprehension. The right balance creates rhythm and guides the reader's eye naturally through the content.

微妙的深度 Subtle Depth

极简排版并不意味着平面设计。精心应用的文字阴影创造微妙的深度，增强可读性，同时不引人注意效果本身。目标是创造一种质量感和意图感。 Minimal typography doesn't mean flat design. Carefully applied text shadows create subtle depth that enhances legibility without drawing attention to the effect itself. The goal is to create a sense of quality and intention.

使用正确时，柔和的阴影将排版从页面中提升出来，创造一种触觉质感，在有意识和潜意识层面上吸引观众。 When used correctly, soft shadows elevate typography from the page, creating a tactile quality that engages viewers on both conscious and subconscious levels.
